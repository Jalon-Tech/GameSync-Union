Boss Fight Strategies

Comprehensive guides, counters, and phase breakdowns for bosses across PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and PC games.

🗡️ General Radahn – Elden Ring (All Platforms)

A massive fight on horseback with endless summons. Stay mobile and summon allies immediately. When he launches himself from the sky, keep moving or hide behind debris.

  • ⚔️ Use Bleed weapons for quick stagger buildup.
  • 🎯 Range users: Hit and back away, don’t get greedy.
  • 🌪️ Phase 2 requires constant camera tracking — be aware of his comet dive.

🕷️ Venom – Spider-Man 2 (PS5)

A brutal multi-phase battle. Focus on dodging heavy combos and using your own symbiote abilities when he’s staggered. Time L1 + Triangle finishers during his berserk phases.

  • 🛡️ Don’t try to parry during rage mode — dodge instead.
  • 🧠 Focus on pure melee combat — gadgets are disabled.
  • 🎬 Watch for QTE prompts that lead to cinematic finishers.

🔥 Ganon – Tears of the Kingdom (Switch)

Armed with multiple phases and corruption attacks. Equip fused weapons and stock up on food and arrows. Learn dodge timing for the flurry rush window.

  • 🔺 Use Recall to reverse Ganon’s projectiles.
  • 🎯 Aim for headshots when airborne for critical damage.
  • 🍴 Cook high-level defense and stamina meals beforehand.

👊 Shao Kahn – Mortal Kombat 1 (Xbox/PS5/PC)

A powerful brute with hyper armor. Use zoning and ranged moves to bait unsafe swings. Never corner yourself — use Fatal Blows wisely.

  • 💥 Avoid his shoulder charge or punish it with fast jabs.
  • 🧊 Sub-Zero & Sindel are good counters due to range.
  • 🧪 Meter burn breakers to interrupt his long combos.
